How it works

This system uses computer vision to detect signs of drowsiness and yawning by analyzing facial landmarks. When signs of fatigue are detected, an alert is triggered.

  • Eye aspect ratio (EAR) is used to detect drowsiness
  • Mouth aspect ratio (MAR) is used to detect yawning
  • Real-time alerts are provided when fatigue is detected
